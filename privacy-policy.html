Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how HorrorVideoBot ("we", "our", "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok Account Information

When you authorize our App, we access:

  • Your TikTok username and user ID
  • Basic profile information (display name, avatar)
  • Permission to upload videos to your account

2.2 Generated Content

We temporarily store:

  • AI-generated stories (text)
  • Generated images (temporary, deleted after upload)
  • Audio narration files (temporary, deleted after upload)

2.3 Technical Information

We may collect:

  • API request logs (for debugging and performance)
  • Error logs and system diagnostics
  • Usage statistics (number of videos generated)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and upload videos to your TikTok account
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality
  • Comply with TikTok's API Terms of Service
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Temporary Files: All generated content (images, audio, videos) is stored temporarily on our servers and automatically deleted within 24 hours.

Access Tokens: TikTok OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We never store your TikTok password.

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your TikTok account credentials
  • Use your content for training AI models (beyond generating your videos)
  • Share your data with advertisers

We MAY share data:

  • With TikTok (as required by their API Terms of Service)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of others

6. Third-Party Services

Our App uses:

  • TikTok API: Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI / Local LLM: For story generation (text only, not shared externally)
  • Stable Diffusion: For image generation (processed locally)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the App from your TikTok account at any time via TikTok settings
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the service at any time

8. Data Retention

  • Generated Content: Deleted within 24 hours of creation
  • Access Tokens: Stored until you revoke access
  • Logs: Kept for 30 days for debugging purposes

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
